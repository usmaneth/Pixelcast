Spatial/Radial Controls

Controls float around the video as spatial pills. Click one to expand it into a panel. Most experimental — nobody does this. Could be genius or could be confusing. High risk, high reward.

Context: The settings panel sits on the right side of the editor. It controls zoom levels, playback speed, cursor effects, backgrounds, export settings, and more. Currently it uses a 4-tab system (Style/Motion/Audio/Export) that works but looks generic.
