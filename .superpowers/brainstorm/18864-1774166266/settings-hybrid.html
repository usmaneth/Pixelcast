Key Ideas in This Hybrid

Spatial Pills

Floating indicator pills near the video showing active zoom/speed. Click to jump to that control in the panel. Like a HUD overlay for your edit.

Contextual Cards

When you select a zoom/speed/trim region on the timeline, a branded card appears at the top of the panel with that region's controls. Deselect = card disappears.

Property Rows

Below the contextual card, clean label + control rows for persistent settings (roundness, padding, shadow). Compact, scannable, mono values.

klipt Red Accents

Active states, selected items, and toggles use #E0000F instead of blue. Switches, active zoom buttons, contextual card borders — all klipt red.
